Shipping Policy

Learn how we process, ship, and deliver your orders at Shoppyness.in.

Shipping Charges

  • ₹50 for parcels up to 500 grams.
  • COD available with an additional ₹50 charge.

Delivery Area

We currently deliver only within India. International shipping is not available.

Delivery Time

  • Orders are delivered within 5–7 working days after dispatch.
  • We dispatch using trusted courier partners based on region and availability.

Order Tracking

Tracking details will be emailed to you within 24–48 hours after shipping.

Tracking details may take up to 24 hours to activate on the courier website.

Address & Contact Requirements

Please ensure your delivery details include:

  • Complete address (Flat No., Street, Area)
  • Correct PIN code
  • Active phone number
  • Valid email ID

Order & Address Changes

Changes can be made before dispatch or within 24 hours of placing the order.

Once shipped, two-way courier charges apply for changes or cancellation.

Delays & Stock Availability

If dispatch is delayed due to stock or courier issues, we will notify you via email.
